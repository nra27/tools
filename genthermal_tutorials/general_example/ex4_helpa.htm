Location of reference points

Which domains to make structural?

Note that all domains except the shaft, IGB & the dummy block should be made structural.  To prevent the model flying into infinity on running, prescribed displacements of 0mm should be applied at the most forward parts of each component as shown below.

Graph Definition

  • Initially, both casing and blade are stationary and have a cold build clearance (cbc) of 0.25mm.

If the casing moves +5mm, and blade moves +2.5mm radially, the gap gets bigger by 2.5mm, so total gap is 5-2.5+0.25.

  • Tip Clearance = {y-displacement casing - y-displacement blade + cbc}

  • A positive number on the graph represents clearance.  Zero represents touching.  As soon as the graph goes negative, the blade and the casing rub.
