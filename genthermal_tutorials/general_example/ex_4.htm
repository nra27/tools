Exercise 4 - Tip Clearance Analysis

Blade tip clearance can be controlled by the selection of component materials with appropriate thermal expansion coefficients (alpha). This problem will examine the effect changing the material has on the temperature and movement of a given component.

Exercise Objectives

To examine the effect of changing the HPT casing from a nickel alloy of high alpha (C1023 [QAB] alpha = 16.20E-6 at 1000 degrees Celsius) to N907 [QRK] (alpha = 14.0E-6 at 1000 degrees Celsius) on the casing temperature and displacement.

Problem

Given the basic model described in the preceding sections, change the HPT casing material from C1023 (QAB) to N907 (QRK), and determine:

  • The change in casing temperature and movements

  • The change in the HPT tip clearance

Instructions

Using the basic SC03 model (example.pm), re-run the model thermo-mechanically and plot:

  • Plot 1:  Graph of HPT casing temperature, casing radial displacement and blade radial displacement as a function of time over the square cycle

  • Plot 2:  Graph of the HPT tip clearance as a function of time

Assume the HPT blade tip cold build clearance (cbc) of 0.25 mm.
