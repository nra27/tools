SC03 Tutorial for 3D-3D Coupling with SC89

11. Coupling results

When the coupling has been completed for the whole flight cycle SC03 will say the Run Completed OK.

You may carry out post-processing on the SC03 results in the normal way.

Your results should resemble the temperature plot on the left.  The range for this contour plot was 471.7-732.8K.

You have completed the SC89 3D-3D tutorial for a simple disc and shaft coupled to two CFD models.
