Hand Calculations

 

A PowerPoint Presentation is available which details a series of hand calculations on the following subjects:

  • Temperatures

    • Conduction

    • Convection

    • Radiation

    • Thermal contact

    • Steady-state scaling

    • Transient scaling

  • Displacements

    • Thermal effects

    • CF effects

    • Pressure effects

    • Tensile/compressive effects

Click here to view the presentation

In each case, there is a page of simple theory, an example calculation, and a simple SC03 model to perform the same calculation.

SC03 Models

Note that the two SC03 models that are referred to (hand_calc.pm and transient_scaling.pm) are both available as zip files (hand_calc.zip and transient_scaling.zip).  Simply right-click on the links and save the zip files to your local area.  This can then be unzipped and used as required.
